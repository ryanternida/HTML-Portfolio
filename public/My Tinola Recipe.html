Tinola Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kilo of Chicken
  • 1 medium size of sayote, sliced into small pieces
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium size of white onion, minced
  • a thumb of ginger, grated
  • Chili leaves
  • Malunggay leaves
  • Chicken stock or boullion
  • 5 cups of water

Steps:

  1. Saute garlic, ginger,and onion until it softens.
  2. Add the chicken. Then cook until chicken has browning on the outside.
  3. Add water and fish sauce. Let it boil.
  4. Add the chicken stock or boullion. Add the sayote and let it simmer.
  5. After 20 minutes, add the chili leaves and the malunggay leaves.
  6. After 5 minutes, add salt and pepper as needed.
